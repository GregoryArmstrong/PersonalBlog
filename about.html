About Greg Armstrong

Greg Armstrong is currently a student at the Turing School of Software & Design in Denver, Colorado.

He was born in Orlando, Florida in 1987 and resided there until 2006 when he moved to Gainesville, Florida to attend the University of Florida.

Go Gators!

Having graduated from UF in 2010, Greg worked at the McKnight Brain Institute in the Shands Medical Complex doing Alzheimer's & Parkinson's research study recruting for the Veteran's Affairs Medical Center as well as spinal cord injury research for the Department of Physical Therapy.

Greg moved to Denver, Colorado in the summer of 2015 after a 9,000 mile road trip through the western United States visiting National Parks such as the Grand Canyon, Zion, Yosemite, Big Sur, Crater Lake, Yellowstone, the Grand Tetons and Arches.
